About me:

During my time attending the University of Kentucky, I have taken many interests in all forms of digital art, ceramics, and photography. I tend to have a focus on creating art as a process to learn a new skill, or develope existing skills, in order to build my knowledge in a variety of different areas.

I was born and raised in Kentucky and I plan on continuing to develope my skills in hopes of building a career in VFX, motion graphics, animation, game development, and/or web design. I also plan to persue ceramics and photography as both a professional skill, and as a personal hobby, given the time.

Please feel free to contact me for any inquiries about my work, process, or for commissions.
